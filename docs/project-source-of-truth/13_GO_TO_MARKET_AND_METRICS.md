# Go-To-Market and Metrics

## Launch wedge

Do not launch as “a poker media platform.”

Launch as:

> The weekly poker clip league.

Alternative copy:

- “Vote for this week’s best poker clip.”
- “Turn poker moments into ranking and reputation.”
- “Post clips. Win the week. Build your poker profile.”

## Founder Season

Run a manual 4-week test.

Targets:

- 20 creators,
- 50 clips,
- 300 votes,
- 100 comments/reactions,
- at least 1 completed weekly contest,
- 3 club/brand sponsor conversations.

## Creator outreach message

```text
Hei! Vi bygger ProPokerTV som en non-gambling poker clip league der creators kan poste highlights, bli stemt frem og bygge pokerstatus.

Vi kjører en liten Founder Season med 20 creators. Hver uke nomineres klipp i kategorier som Best Bluff, Sickest River, Funniest Poker Moment og Clip of the Week.

Vil du være med som early creator? Målet er enkelt: post 1–2 pokerklipp per uke, få votes, og konkurrer om weekly winner-status.
```

## Partner outreach message

```text
Hei! Vi tester ProPokerTV Weekly Poker Clip League — en non-gambling highlight contest for pokerklipp.

Vi ser etter én pokerklubb/event som vil teste en enkel partner-contest: “Club Highlight of the Week powered by ProPokerTV.”

Dere får en branded contest, synlighet for klubben og en enkel måte å aktivere communityet rundt highlights og voting.
```

## Initial contest categories

- Clip of the Week
- Best Bluff
- Sickest River
- Funniest Poker Moment
- Best Hand Breakdown

## Product metrics

| Metric | Meaning | Target |
|---|---|---:|
| creator_signed_up | Supply activation | 20 |
| creator_profile_completed | Creator commitment | 15 |
| clip_created | Supply behavior | 50 in 30 days |
| clip_approved | Moderated supply | 40+ |
| clip_nominated | Contest supply | 20+ |
| contest_vote_cast | Fan participation | 300 |
| unique_voters | Voting reach | 100+ |
| comments_created | Community | 50+ |
| reactions_created | Lightweight engagement | 50+ |
| creator_repeat_posted | Creator retention | 30% |
| contest_finalized | Loop completion | 1 minimum |
| partner_interest_yes | Commercial signal | 3 |

## Most important metric

The most important early metric is:

> How many creators post again without being chased?

## Weekly operating cadence

Every week:

1. Recruit creators.
2. Approve clips.
3. Nominate best clips.
4. Push fan voting.
5. Finalize winner.
6. Announce winner.
7. Update leaderboard/badges.
8. Ask creators for next week’s clips.
9. Review metrics.
10. Improve one friction point.

## Partner test

First commercial test:

> “Club X Highlight of the Week powered by ProPokerTV.”

Do not build a full partner dashboard first.

Manual partner pilot acceptance criteria:

- club/event agrees to promote contest,
- at least 5 clips submitted,
- at least 50 votes,
- winner announced publicly,
- partner asks about repeating or sponsoring.
