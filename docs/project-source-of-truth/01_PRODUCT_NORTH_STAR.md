# Product North Star

## One-liner

ProPokerTV helps poker creators and players build reputation through weekly highlight contests.

## End positioning

ProPokerTV is the non-gambling poker media and reputation layer for the global poker community.

## MVP positioning

The weekly poker clip league.

## What the product is

ProPokerTV is not just a video feed.

It is a status engine for poker moments:

- creators post poker highlights,
- fans vote and react,
- weekly contests create winners,
- winners create reputation,
- profiles and leaderboards make reputation persistent,
- clubs/events/brands can later sponsor contests and creator opportunities.

## What users should understand in 3 seconds

> Vote for this week’s best poker clip.

## Core promise

Creators get a reason to post because ProPokerTV turns poker clips into visible status.

Fans get a reason to return because they can influence who wins.

Partners get a reason to pay later because contests, rankings, and creators create a targeted poker media surface.

## Product category

Primary category:

> Poker Creator League

Supporting categories:

- Poker Media Network
- Poker Reputation Platform
- Global Poker Highlight Network
- Non-gambling poker community platform

## Product principles

1. Weekly contest first.
2. Reputation must be persistent.
3. No gambling or betting functionality.
4. Media is the attention layer; identity and competition are the moat.
5. Every feature must strengthen at least one loop:
   - creator posts more,
   - fan returns to vote,
   - reputation/status increases,
   - platform becomes safer,
   - partner has reason to pay,
   - ProPokerTV becomes harder to copy.

## Product non-negotiables

- The homepage must make the active contest obvious.
- Creator profiles must show status, not just biography.
- Completed contests must produce persistent winner history.
- Voting must be protected from trivial abuse.
- Moderation must exist before broad public launch.
- The platform must remain non-gambling.
